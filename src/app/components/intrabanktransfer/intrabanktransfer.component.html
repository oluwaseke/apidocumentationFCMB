Intrabank Transfer API

Intrabank Transfer API Endpoints

B2C Transfer

Enables a business entity to transfer money from their account to FCMB customers. The account of the business must have been configured by the bank against the clientID. Fee is usually associated with this API based on agreement with the bank.


Sample Request:

POST https://devapi.fcmb.com/intrabanktransferopenapi/api/IntrabankTransfer/b2cTransfer HTTP/1.1
client_id: 250
Content-Type: application/json
Cache-Control: no-cache
Ocp-Apim-Subscription-Key: 32e9c71e2f3b45a3a248f43e32bdbe1e
{{ "{" }}
"debitAccountNo": "1289907027",
"creditAccountNo": "1289907010",
"amount": 1000,
"productId": 11,
"narration": "Test transaction",
"debitMerchantForCharge": true,
"requestId": "1234567720118843"
}
